Cugar v 0.9 (first release)
===========================
Frontend for SugarCRM and CAO-Faktura.

* Supports inserts for contacts and companys simultaniously in SugarCRM and CAO-Faktura.

* Supports updating for contacts and companys simultaniously in SugarCRM and CAO-Faktura.

Download SugarCRM @ http://www.sugarcrm.com
Download CAO-Faktura @ http://www.cmr-faktura.de/index.php/caosupport/cao-downloads
